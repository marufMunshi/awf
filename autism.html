What is Autism

Autism

Autism is a complex development disability that typically appears during the first three years of life and affects a person’s ability to communication and interacts with others. [Autism is spectrum disorder, which means the condition has wide ranging degrees of severity]There is also a condition called Asperser Syndrome which is a form of autism used to describe people at the higher functioning end of the Autism Spectrum.

Characteristics of Autism

The degree or characteristics differ from person to person, but all those affected have impairment in Social interaction, social communication, repetitive and restricted patterns of behavior. This is referred to as a triad of impairment.
Social Communication (Difficulty with verbal and non-verbal communication)
Social Interaction (Difficulty with social relationship)
Behavior (Repetitive and restricted patterns of behavior)
In addition to this triad, resistance to change in routine is a notable feature Individual with autism may also experiences sensivity in the senses of sight, hearing, touch, smell, movement.

Causes of Autism

Autism is a brain disorder which affects the way brain uses information. The exact cause or causes of autism is/are still unknown. Research shows that genetic factors are important. Autism may indeed result from a combination of several ‘’causes’’. [Autism is a brain based development disorder. No factors in the psychological environment of the Child cause autism]
